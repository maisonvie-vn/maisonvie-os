# TASK-0017 — Guest Feedback Foundation

## Objective
The objective of TASK-0017 is to build the **Guest Feedback Foundation** inside MVOS Studio. This enables recording guest complaints, compliments, suggestions, and service incident records, mapping them to reservation logs, daily reports, learning lessons, or corrective actions to close the operational feedback loop.

## Data Structure

### Guest Feedback Record Fields
* `id` (UUID): Unique feedback identifier.
* `feedbackDate` (DATE): Date feedback was received.
* `visitDate` (DATE, Nullable): Date of dining visit.
* `guestName` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `guestContact` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `sourceChannel` (VARCHAR): Channel enums (`onsite`, `email`, `phone`, `zalo`, `google_review`, `tripadvisor`, `facebook`, `tour_guide`, `travel_agency`, `manager_note`, `other`).
* `feedbackType` (VARCHAR): Feedback enums (`complaint`, `compliment`, `suggestion`, `incident`, `service_issue`, `kitchen_issue`, `reservation_issue`, `email_issue`, `review`, `other`).
* `rating` (NUMERIC, Nullable): Guest score from 1 to 5.
* `severity` (VARCHAR): Severity enums (`low`, `medium`, `high`, `critical`).
* `status` (VARCHAR): Lifecycle status (`new`, `reviewing`, `action_required`, `resolved`, `archived`).
* `title` (VARCHAR): Short title.
* `content` (TEXT): Detailed guest feedback.
* `reservationId` (UUID, Nullable).
* `dailyReportId` (UUID, Nullable).
* `learningEventId` (UUID, Nullable).
* `improvementActionId` (UUID, Nullable).
* `ownerName` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `actionRequired` (BOOLEAN): Repair/action required flag.
* `ceoAttentionRequired` (BOOLEAN): CEO attention flag.
* `resolutionNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `resolvedAt` (TIMESTAMP, Nullable).

## Features Implemented

### 1. Summary Cards
Shows real statistics calculated from the feedback records list:
* **Tổng phản hồi**: Total feedbacks logged.
* **Phản hồi mới**: Feedbacks with status `new`.
* **Cần hành động**: Feedbacks with `actionRequired = true` and unresolved status.
* **Cần CEO xem**: Feedbacks marked with CEO attention flag and unresolved.
* **Đã xử lý**: Resolved feedbacks.

### 2. Feedback Form
Allows logging feedback details, rating scores, severity, guest name/contacts, and linking records to reservations, reports, learning events, and corrective actions.

### 3. Detail & State Action Panel
Displays complete feedback details and allows updating status (`Chuyển sang đang xem xét`, `Đánh dấu cần hành động`, `Đánh dấu đã xử lý` with custom resolution notes, and `Lưu trữ phản hồi`).

## Out of Scope & Limitations
* **Review Scraping**: No automated API scraping or fetching reviews from TripAdvisor, Google, or Facebook.
* **AI Sentiment Analysis**: No AI sentiment scoring or automated guest replies.
* **Supabase Integration**: Currently stores changes to `localStorage` for mocking capabilities. Database triggers and real client queries will be wired in future development stages.
