## Objective
The objective of TASK-0032 is to implement the **Unit Conversion Foundation** in MVOS Studio. This enables Maison Vie to define global conversions (e.g. kg to g, liter to ml) and ingredient-specific conversions (e.g. 1 box of lobster = 5 kg), supporting preview conversions without mutating historical data or automatically normalizing existing inventory stock balances.

## Data Structure

### Unit Conversion Fields
* `id` (UUID): Unique conversion config identifier.
* `ingredientMasterId` (UUID, Nullable): Reference to Ingredient Master (required only for ingredient-specific).
* `fromUnit` (VARCHAR): Source unit to convert from.
* `toUnit` (VARCHAR): Target unit to convert to.
* `factor` (NUMERIC): Conversion factor (multiplication factor: `converted_quantity = original_quantity * factor`).
* `conversionType` (VARCHAR): Type enum (`global`, `ingredient_specific`).
* `isActive` (BOOLEAN): Status toggle.
* `notes` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `createdAt` (TIMESTAMPTZ).
* `updatedAt` (TIMESTAMPTZ).

## Out of Scope & Limitations
* **Automatic Conversions**: Existing recipes, stock balances, purchase orders, and goods receipts are not automatically modified or converted.
* **Costing & WAC**: No valuation, WAC calculation, food cost calculations, or accounting integrations.
* **Multi-Branch Readiness**: No branch ownership, multi-branch filters, or branch permissions. RFC-0010 remains Proposed.
* **Supabase Integration**: Stores changes to `localStorage` for mocking capabilities.
