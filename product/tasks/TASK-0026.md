# TASK-0026 — Supplier Master Foundation

## Objective
The objective of TASK-0026 is to implement the **Supplier Master Foundation** in MVOS Studio. This enables Maison Vie to manage a structured master list of suppliers and basic supplier ingredient capabilities.

---

## Data Structure

### Supplier Master Fields
* `id` (UUID): Unique supplier identifier.
* `supplierCode` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Supplier code (e.g., SUP-HAIPHONG-FOOD).
* `supplierName` (VARCHAR): Full name of the supplier.
* `supplierType` (VARCHAR): Supplier type enum (`food_supplier`, `beverage_supplier`, `wine_supplier`, `seafood_supplier`, `meat_supplier`, `vegetable_supplier`, `dairy_supplier`, `dry_goods_supplier`, `bakery_supplier`, `equipment_supplier`, `cleaning_supplier`, `service_provider`, `other`).
* `status` (VARCHAR): Status enum (`prospect`, `active`, `paused`, `inactive`, `blocked`, `archived`).
* `priority` (VARCHAR): Priority enum (`low`, `medium`, `high`, `strategic`).
* `contactName` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Main contact person.
* `contactRole` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Role of contact person.
* `contactEmail` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Contact email address.
* `contactPhone` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Contact phone number.
* `address` (TEXT, Nullable): Supplier business address.
* `website` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `taxCode` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Business tax identifier code.
* `invoiceNote` (TEXT, Nullable): Invoicing/VAT rules.
* `deliveryNote` (TEXT, Nullable): Logistical/delivery rules.
* `paymentTermNote` (TEXT, Nullable): Payment/credit term rules.
* `qualityNote` (TEXT, Nullable): Quality standards.
* `storageHandlingNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `categoryNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `internalNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `ownerName` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Person responsible.
* `lastContactDate` (DATE, Nullable).
* `nextReviewDate` (DATE, Nullable).
* `createdAt` (TIMESTAMPTZ).
* `updatedAt` (TIMESTAMPTZ).

### Supplier Ingredient Capability Fields
* `id` (UUID): Unique capability identifier.
* `supplierId` (UUID): Reference to supplier.
* `ingredientMasterId` (UUID, Nullable): Optional reference to Ingredient Master.
* `ingredientCategory` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Optional fallback category if no specific item is linked.
* `capabilityNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `qualityNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `leadTimeNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `minimumOrderNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `status` (VARCHAR): Status enum (`draft`, `active`, `paused`, `archived`).
* `createdAt` (TIMESTAMPTZ).
* `updatedAt` (TIMESTAMPTZ).

## Out of Scope & Limitations
* **Purchase Orders**: No purchase orders, procurement workflows, or supplier approval workflows.
* **Supplier Pricing**: No supplier price lists, price history, or contract pricing.
* **Costing & WAC**: No ingredient unit cost, WAC calculations, food cost, or recipe rollup.
* **Inventory & Stock**: No inventory integrations, stock counts, stock movements, or par stocks.
* **Accounting & Payments**: No invoice management, debt tracking, payment tracking, VAT calculations, or accounting integrations.
* **Supplier Scoring**: No supplier performance analytics or supplier scoring.
* **AI & Optimization**: No AI supplier recommendations, forecasting, or production planning.
* **Multi-Branch Readiness**: No branch ownership, multi-branch filters, or branch permissions. RFC-0010 remains Proposed.
* **Supabase Integration**: Stores changes to `localStorage` for mocking capabilities.
