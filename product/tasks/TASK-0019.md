# TASK-0019 — Travel Agency Partner Foundation

## Objective
The objective of TASK-0019 is to build the **Travel Agency Partner Database** inside MVOS Studio. This enables structured tracking of travel agencies, tour operators, corporate clients, key contacts, preferred languages, and policy terms (VAT, FOC, discounts, menu preferences) without introducing pricing calculations, commission workflows, or invoice generation.

---

## Data Structure

### Travel Agency Partner Fields
* `id` (UUID): Unique partner identifier.
* `partnerName` (VARCHAR): Agency name.
* `partnerCode` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Short code.
* `partnerType` (VARCHAR): Type enums (`travel_agency`, `tour_operator`, `dmc`, `corporate_client`, `hotel_concierge`, `event_planner`, `other`).
* `market` (VARCHAR): Market enums (`japan`, `korea`, `europe`, `france`, `usa`, `australia`, `vietnam`, `southeast_asia`, `global`, `other`).
* `status` (VARCHAR): Partner status (`prospect`, `active`, `inactive`, `paused`, `blacklisted`, `archived`).
* `priority` (VARCHAR): Priority enums (`low`, `medium`, `high`, `strategic`).
* `contactName` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `contactRole` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `contactEmail` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `contactPhone` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `preferredLanguage` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `address` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `website` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `bookingPolicyNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `menuPolicyNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `paymentPolicyNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `vatPolicyNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `focPolicyNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `surchargePolicyNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `allergyPolicyNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `internalNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `ownerName` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `lastContactDate` (DATE, Nullable).
* `nextFollowUpDate` (DATE, Nullable).

---

## Out of Scope & Limitations
* **Pricing Engine**: No automatic discounting, FOC calculations, VAT calculations, or menu price lookups. All policies are stored as plaintext notes.
* **CRM Automation**: No email campaign scheduling or contact sync.
* **Supabase Integration**: Currently stores changes to `localStorage` for mocking capabilities. Database triggers and real client queries will be wired in future development stages.
