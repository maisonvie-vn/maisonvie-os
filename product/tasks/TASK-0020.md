# TASK-0020 — Tour Group Booking Operations Foundation

## Objective
The objective of TASK-0020 is to build the **Tour Group Booking Operations Foundation** inside MVOS Studio. This enables structured tracking and operational management of incoming tour group reservations, food allergies, FOH setup directives, and BOH kitchen preparation notes, mapped to partner accounts and dining reservation logs.

## Data Structure

### Tour Group Booking Fields
* `id` (UUID): Unique group booking identifier.
* `agencyPartnerId` (UUID, Nullable): References travel agency partner.
* `reservationId` (UUID, Nullable): References standard reservation.
* `tourCode` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Code of the tour/itinerary.
* `bookingReference` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `groupName` (VARCHAR): Name of the tour group.
* `market` (VARCHAR): Market enums (`japan`, `korea`, `europe`, `france`, `usa`, `australia`, `vietnam`, `southeast_asia`, `global`, `other`).
* `guestCount` (INTEGER): Estimated pax count.
* `confirmedGuestCount` (INTEGER, Nullable): Final chốt khách count.
* `arrivalDate` (DATE).
* `arrivalTime` (TIME).
* `departureTime` (TIME, Nullable).
* `menuName` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `menuNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `allergyNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `specialRequestNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `guideName` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `guidePhone` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `leaderName` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `leaderPhone` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `floorTableNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `kitchenNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `serviceNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `status` (VARCHAR): Booking status (`inquiry`, `tentative`, `confirmed`, `changed`, `cancelled`, `completed`, `archived`).
* `operationStatus` (VARCHAR): Operations readiness (`not_ready`, `preparing`, `ready`, `in_service`, `completed`, `issue`).
* `ownerName` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `confirmedAt` (TIMESTAMP, Nullable).

---

## Out of Scope & Limitations
* **Pricing/Invoicing Engine**: No calculations for group prices, FOC rules, VAT surcharges, or travel agency commissions. All terms are tracked via plaintext notes.
* **Email & Communication**: No automatic notifications or drafts sent to partners or guides.
* **Supabase Integration**: Currently stores changes to `localStorage` for mocking capabilities. Database triggers and real client queries will be wired in future development stages.
