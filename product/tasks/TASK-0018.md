# TASK-0018 — Guest Recovery & Follow-up Foundation

## Objective
The objective of TASK-0018 is to build the **Guest Recovery & Follow-up Foundation** inside MVOS Studio. This enables recording guest recovery cases, linking them to feedback records, reservations, email drafts, or improvement actions to close the guest experience loop.

## Data Structure

### Guest Recovery Case Fields
* `id` (UUID): Unique recovery case identifier.
* `feedbackId` (UUID, Nullable): Associated feedback record.
* `reservationId` (UUID, Nullable): Associated reservation.
* `emailDraftId` (UUID, Nullable): Associated email draft.
* `improvementActionId` (UUID, Nullable): Associated improvement action.
* `caseDate` (DATE): Date the case was opened.
* `guestName` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `guestContact` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `recoveryType` (VARCHAR): Recovery enums (`apology`, `manager_call`, `email_follow_up`, `agency_follow_up`, `complimentary_item`, `discount_offer`, `rebooking_offer`, `service_correction`, `internal_resolution`, `other`).
* `priority` (VARCHAR): Priority enums (`low`, `medium`, `high`, `critical`).
* `status` (VARCHAR): Lifecycle status (`open`, `contacting`, `waiting_guest_response`, `recovery_offered`, `resolved`, `closed`, `archived`).
* `ownerName` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `dueDate` (DATE, Nullable).
* `issueSummary` (TEXT): Description of the incident.
* `recoveryPlan` (TEXT, Nullable): Proposed gesture.
* `followUpNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `recoveryResult` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `ceoAttentionRequired` (BOOLEAN): Requires CEO approval for large gestures.
* `resolvedAt` (TIMESTAMP, Nullable).

## Features Implemented

### 1. Summary Cards
Shows real statistics calculated from the recovery cases list:
* **Tổng case**: Total recovery cases logged.
* **Đang xử lý**: Cases currently in-progress (`open`, `contacting`, `recovery_offered`).
* **Chờ phản hồi khách**: Cases with status `waiting_guest_response`.
* **Cần CEO xem**: Cases requiring CEO approval for compensation.
* **Đã xử lý**: Cases that are resolved or closed.

### 2. Case Form
Allows logging case details, priority, recovery type, due date, owner, and linking to feedback, reservation, email drafts, or improvement actions.

### 3. Detail & State Action Panel
Displays complete case details and allows updating status (`Chuyển sang đang liên hệ`, `Đánh dấu chờ phản hồi khách`, `Đánh dấu đã đề xuất phục hồi`, `Đánh dấu đã xử lý` with custom resolution notes, and `Lưu trữ case`).

## Out of Scope & Limitations
* **Review Scraping**: No automated API scraping or fetching reviews.
* **AI Communication**: No AI sentiment analysis or auto-replies.
* **Loyalty / CRM**: This is not a loyalty management tool or full CRM.
* **Supabase Integration**: Currently stores changes to `localStorage` for mocking capabilities. Database triggers and real client queries will be wired in future development stages.
