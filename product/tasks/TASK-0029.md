# TASK-0029 — Goods Receipt Foundation

## Objective
The objective of TASK-0029 is to implement the **Goods Receipt Foundation** in MVOS Studio. This enables Maison Vie to record actual physical items received from suppliers against Purchase Orders, supporting partial receiving, damaged or rejected quantities, and issue tracking.

## Data Structure

### Goods Receipt Header Fields
* `id` (UUID): Unique goods receipt identifier.
* `purchaseOrderId` (UUID): Reference to parent purchase order.
* `receiptNumber` (VARCHAR): Unique receipt number (e.g., GR-20260630-01).
* `supplierId` (UUID, Nullable): Reference to supplier.
* `receivedDate` (DATE): Received date.
* `receivedBy` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Person who received the goods.
* `status` (VARCHAR): Status enum (`draft`, `received`, `partially_received`, `issue_reported`, `cancelled`).
* `notes` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `createdAt` (TIMESTAMPTZ).
* `updatedAt` (TIMESTAMPTZ).

### Goods Receipt Item Fields
* `id` (UUID): Unique item line identifier.
* `goodsReceiptId` (UUID): Reference to parent goods receipt.
* `purchaseOrderLineId` (UUID, Nullable): Reference to source purchase order line.
* `ingredientMasterId` (UUID, Nullable): Reference to Ingredient Master.
* `orderedQuantity` (NUMERIC, Nullable).
* `receivedQuantity` (NUMERIC): Received quantity.
* `acceptedQuantity` (NUMERIC): Accepted quantity.
* `rejectedQuantity` (NUMERIC): Rejected quantity.
* `damagedQuantity` (NUMERIC): Damaged quantity.
* `unit` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `unitPrice` (NUMERIC, Nullable).
* `issueNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `createdAt` (TIMESTAMPTZ).
* `updatedAt` (TIMESTAMPTZ).

## Out of Scope & Limitations
* **Stock & Inventory**: No inventory stock level calculation, par stocks, stock movements, or stock ledger records.
* **Pricing & WAC**: No WAC calculation, unit cost updates, food cost calculations, or recipe costing updates.
* **Invoicing & Payments**: No invoice management, payment tracking, accounting integration, or VAT calculations.
* **Multi-Branch Readiness**: No branch ownership, multi-branch filters, or branch permissions. RFC-0010 remains Proposed.
* **Supabase Integration**: Stores changes to `localStorage` for mocking capabilities.
