# TASK-0016 — Manager Daily Report Foundation

## Objective
The objective of TASK-0016 is to build the **Manager Daily Report Foundation** inside MVOS Studio. This enables restaurant managers to log structured shift summaries, guest feedback, FOH/BOH service issues, maintenance issues, staff notes, and CEO attention flags to establish consistent operational transparency.

## Data Structure

### Daily Operation Report Fields
* `id` (UUID): Unique report identifier.
* `reportDate` (DATE): Date of shift/day.
* `shiftType` (VARCHAR): Shift enums (`lunch`, `dinner`, `full_day`, `event`, `other`).
* `department` (VARCHAR): Target department (`foh`, `boh`, `reservation`, `email`, `management`, `all`, `other`).
* `managerName` (VARCHAR): Manager compiling the report.
* `status` (VARCHAR): Lifecycle status (`draft`, `submitted`, `reviewed`, `archived`).
* `overallSummary` (TEXT): Overall operational summary.
* `guestNotes` (TEXT, Nullable): Guest complaints or compliments.
* `serviceIssues` / `kitchenIssues` / `reservationIssues` / `emailIssues` / `maintenanceIssues` / `staffNotes` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `checklistRunId` (UUID, Nullable): Associated checklist execution run.
* `improvementActionId` (UUID, Nullable): Associated improvement corrective action.
* `actionRequired` (BOOLEAN): Repair/action required flag.
* `ceoAttentionRequired` (BOOLEAN): Escalation attention flag.
* `submittedAt` / `reviewedAt` (TIMESTAMP, Nullable).

## Features Implemented

### 1. Summary Cards
Shows real statistics calculated from the reports list:
* **Báo cáo hôm nay**: Reports submitted/drafted today.
* **Đã gửi**: Reports with status `submitted` or `reviewed`.
* **Bản nháp**: Draft reports.
* **Cần CEO xem**: Reports marked with the CEO attention flag.
* **Có việc cần xử lý**: Reports marked with the corrective action required flag.

### 2. Structured Report Form
Allows logging shift reports with distinct textareas for FOH, BOH, Reservation/Email, Maintenance, and Staff Discipline. Links directly to Checklist runs and Improvement corrective actions.

### 3. Detail & State Action Panel
Displays complete report details and allows updating lifecycle status (`Lưu bản nháp`, `Gửi báo cáo`, `Đánh dấu đã xem`, and `Lưu trữ báo cáo`).

## Out of Scope & Limitations
* **Revenue/Financial Reporting**: No cash drawer matching, POS data integrations, food/labor cost inputs, or profit metrics.
* **AI Report Generation**: No AI-powered text summaries or automated reporting.
* **Supabase Integration**: Currently stores changes to `localStorage` for mocking capabilities. Database triggers and real client queries will be wired in future development stages.
