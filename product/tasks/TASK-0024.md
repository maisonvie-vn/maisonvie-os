# TASK-0024 — Ingredient Master Foundation

## Objective
The objective of TASK-0024 is to implement the **Ingredient Master Foundation** in MVOS Studio. This enables structured tracking and standardization of all ingredients and raw materials used in Maison Vie kitchens, providing default units, storage requirements, allergy notes, quality specs, BOH prep directions, and status tracking. It also integrates an optional reference link connecting recipe ingredient lines directly to the Ingredient Master records.

## Data Structure

### Ingredient Master Item Fields
* `id` (UUID): Unique ingredient identifier.
* `ingredientCode` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Code of the ingredient.
* `ingredientNameVi` (VARCHAR): Vietnamese name of the ingredient.
* `ingredientNameEn` (VARCHAR, Nullable): English name.
* `ingredientNameFr` (VARCHAR, Nullable): French name.
* `category` (VARCHAR): Category enums (`protein`, `beef`, `poultry`, `pork`, `lamb`, `seafood`, `fish`, `shellfish`, `vegetable`, `fruit`, `dairy`, `cheese`, `butter`, `egg`, `dry_goods`, `flour`, `rice_grain`, `pasta`, `spice`, `herb`, `oil_fat`, `sauce`, `stock`, `garnish`, `pastry`, `chocolate`, `beverage`, `wine`, `non_food`, `other`).
* `subcategory` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Secondary sub-group.
* `defaultUnit` (VARCHAR): Storage unit enums (`g`, `kg`, `ml`, `l`, `pcs`, `portion`, `bunch`, `bottle`, `can`, `pack`, `box`, `tray`, `bag`, `other`).
* `purchaseUnitNote` (TEXT, Nullable): Notes on purchase units.
* `recipeUnitNote` (TEXT, Nullable): Notes on recipe usage units.
* `storageType` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Storage condition enums (`dry`, `chilled`, `frozen`, `ambient`, `wine_cellar`, `chemical_safe`, `other`).
* `shelfLifeNote` (TEXT, Nullable): Standard shelf life.
* `allergyNote` (TEXT, Nullable): Allergen risk notes.
* `dietaryNote` (TEXT, Nullable): Dietary options.
* `qualitySpecification` (TEXT, Nullable): Purchase quality specs.
* `preparationNote` (TEXT, Nullable): Prep guidelines.
* `kitchenNote` (TEXT, Nullable): Chef's operational kitchen note.
* `status` (VARCHAR): Item status (`draft`, `active`, `paused`, `archived`).
* `ownerName` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Chef or staff responsible.

## Out of Scope & Limitations
* **Food Cost & rollup**: No unit cost tracking, WAC valuation, or recipe costing formulas.
* **Inventory Stock levels**: No link to real-time physical store inventory, purchase planning, or stock card movement.
* **POS Integration**: No sync to POS sale item codes.
* **AI Assistance**: No AI ingredient normalization or recommendations.
* **Supabase Integration**: Currently stores changes to `localStorage` for mocking capabilities. Database triggers and real client queries will be wired in future development stages.
