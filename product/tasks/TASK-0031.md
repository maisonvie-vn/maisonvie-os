# TASK-0031 — Inventory Stock Balance Foundation

## Objective
The objective of TASK-0031 is to implement the **Inventory Stock Balance Foundation** in MVOS Studio. This enables Maison Vie to view the read-only, real-time stock balance aggregated from inventory movements grouped by ingredient master and unit without storing a separate mutable stock table.

## Data Structure

### Stock Balance View Fields
* `ingredient_master_id` (UUID): Reference to Ingredient Master.
* `unit` (VARCHAR): Grouping unit.
* `total_in_quantity` (NUMERIC): Sum of all stock-in movements.
* `total_out_quantity` (NUMERIC): Sum of all stock-out movements.
* `total_adjustment_quantity` (NUMERIC): Sum of adjustment movements.
* `current_quantity` (NUMERIC): Derived current stock count.
* `last_movement_date` (DATE): Latest date of movement.
* `movement_count` (BIGINT): Total movements logged.

---

## Out of Scope & Limitations
* **Unit Conversions**: Aggregation groups directly by unit name; no automatic conversion between `kg` / `g` / `pack` is performed. Multiple units are listed in separate rows with a Vietnamese badge indicator.
* **Costing & WAC**: No costing calculations, food cost, WAC, or recipe rollup are implemented.
* **Direct Stock Mutation**: The stock balance is strictly read-only; stock corrections can only be made by logging a new inventory movement.
* **Warehouse / Locations**: Single stock container assumption.
* **Multi-Branch Readiness**: No branch ownership, multi-branch filters, or branch permissions. RFC-0010 remains Proposed.
* **Supabase Integration**: Stores changes to `localStorage` for mocking capabilities.
