# TASK-0021 — Private Dining & Event Operations Foundation

## Objective
The objective of TASK-0021 is to build the **Private Dining & Event Operations Foundation** inside MVOS Studio. This enables structured tracking and operational management of incoming private dining, corporate, birthday, anniversary, wine dinner, and special group events, including host info, room setup, decor directives, AV/equipment, kitchen/service notes, and operational status controls.

## Data Structure

### Private Dining Event Fields
* `id` (UUID): Unique event identifier.
* `reservationId` (UUID, Nullable): References standard reservation.
* `agencyPartnerId` (UUID, Nullable): References travel agency partner.
* `checklistRunId` (UUID, Nullable): References daily operations checklist run.
* `eventCode` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Code of the event.
* `eventName` (VARCHAR): Name of the event.
* `eventType` (VARCHAR): Event type enums (`private_dining`, `corporate`, `birthday`, `anniversary`, `proposal`, `wine_dinner`, `banquet`, `meeting`, `family_event`, `press_event`, `other`).
* `hostName` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Guest name/host.
* `hostContact` (VARCHAR, Nullable): SĐT/email of host.
* `companyName` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Company name.
* `guestCount` (INTEGER): Estimated pax count.
* `confirmedGuestCount` (INTEGER, Nullable): Final chốt khách count.
* `eventDate` (DATE).
* `startTime` (TIME).
* `endTime` (TIME, Nullable).
* `roomArea` (VARCHAR): Room enums (`floor_1`, `vip_1`, `vip_2`, `vip_3`, `vip_4`, `floor_3`, `full_restaurant`, `offsite`, `other`).
* `setupStyle` (VARCHAR): Setup style enums (`standard_dining`, `u_shape`, `long_table`, `round_table`, `cocktail`, `theater`, `classroom`, `buffet_style`, `custom`).
* `menuName` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `menuNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `beverageNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `allergyNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `specialRequestNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `decorationNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `cakeNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `flowerNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `avEquipmentNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `kitchenNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `serviceNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `status` (VARCHAR): Booking status (`inquiry`, `tentative`, `confirmed`, `changed`, `cancelled`, `completed`, `archived`).
* `operationStatus` (VARCHAR): Operations readiness (`not_ready`, `preparing`, `ready`, `in_service`, `completed`, `issue`).
* `ownerName` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `confirmedAt` (TIMESTAMP, Nullable).

## Out of Scope & Limitations
* **Pricing/Invoicing Engine**: No calculations for room rental fees, minimum spend requirements, menu prices, or travel agency commissions. All terms are tracked via plaintext notes.
* **Quotations & Contract**: No quotation drafting, signature flow, or contract archiving.
* **Email & Communication**: No automatic notifications or drafts sent to hosts, managers, or kitchen team.
* **Supabase Integration**: Currently stores changes to `localStorage` for mocking capabilities. Database triggers and real client queries will be wired in future development stages.
