# TASK-0014 — SOP Training & Acknowledgement Foundation

## Objective
The objective of TASK-0014 is to build the **SOP Training & Acknowledgement Foundation** inside MVOS Studio. This enables assigning SOP documents to staff members, roles, or departments and tracking their reading and acknowledgement statuses, helping standardise fine dining operations.

## Data Structure

### SOP Training Assignment Fields
* `id` (UUID): Unique training identifier.
* `sopDocumentId` (UUID): Reference to the assigned `sop_documents` record.
* `assigneeName` (VARCHAR): Name of the staff member receiving the training.
* `roleName` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Job role of the staff member.
* `department` (VARCHAR): Department enums (`foh`, `boh`, `reservation`, `email`, etc.).
* `status` (VARCHAR): Training assignment status (`assigned`, `in_progress`, `acknowledged`, `overdue`, `cancelled`).
* `dueDate` (DATE, Nullable): Acknowledgement deadline.
* `acknowledgedAt` (TIMESTAMP, Nullable): Timestamp of acknowledgement.
* `acknowledgementNote` (TEXT, Nullable): Staff member's feedback notes upon acknowledgement.
* `createdAt` / `updatedAt` (TIMESTAMP).

## Features Implemented

### 1. Summary Cards
Shows real statistics calculated from the assignments list:
* **Tổng phân công**: Total training assignments logged.
* **Đã giao**: Assignments with status `assigned`.
* **Đang đọc**: Assignments with status `in_progress`.
* **Đã xác nhận**: Completed assignments with status `acknowledged`.
* **Quá hạn**: Unacknowledged assignments past their due date.

### 2. Assignment Registration Form
Allows logging new training assignments referencing existing SOP documents. Includes input validations in Vietnamese.

### 3. Detailed View & Action Panel
Displays assignments details, due dates, and staff feedback notes, and allows updating assignment status (`Chuyển sang đang đọc`, `Đánh dấu quá hạn`, `Xác nhận hoàn thành` with comments, and `Hủy phân công`).

---

## Out of Scope & Limitations
* **Full LMS & Staff Database**: No full learning management systems, quiz modules, certificates, or staff profiles database.
* **AI Training Recommendations**: No automated training recommendations or AI suggestions.
* **Supabase Integration**: Currently stores changes to `localStorage` for mocking capabilities. Database triggers and real client queries will be wired in future development stages.
