# TASK-0025 — Recipe Ingredient Normalization Foundation

## Objective
The objective of TASK-0025 is to implement the **Recipe Ingredient Normalization Foundation** in MVOS Studio. This links recipe ingredient lines to Ingredient Master items, tracks normalization status (`unmapped`, `mapped`, `needs_review`, `ignored`), standardizes recipe units, and establishes a unit conversion reference table for future cost and inventory evaluation.

## Data Structure

### Extended Recipe Ingredient Line Fields
* `normalizedQuantity` (NUMERIC, Nullable): Quantity of ingredient in normalized default unit.
* `normalizedUnit` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Standardized default unit.
* `normalizationStatus` (VARCHAR): Normalization status (`unmapped`, `mapped`, `needs_review`, `ignored`).
* `normalizationNote` (TEXT, Nullable): Explanatory note or conversion formula notes.

### Unit Conversion Rule Fields
* `id` (UUID): Unique conversion rule identifier.
* `fromUnit` (VARCHAR): Originating unit enums.
* `toUnit` (VARCHAR): Standardized target unit enums.
* `conversionFactor` (NUMERIC): Conversion ratio multiplier.
* `ingredientMasterId` (UUID, Nullable): Optional foreign key to a specific ingredient. If empty, the rule applies globally.
* `note` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `status` (VARCHAR): Status of rule (`draft`, `active`, `paused`, `archived`).

## Out of Scope & Limitations
* **Food Cost & rollup**: No recipe ingredient pricing, margin tracking, or yield loss costing.
* **Inventory Stock levels**: No link to real-time physical store inventory, purchase planning, or stock card movement.
* **POS Integration**: No sync to POS sale item codes.
* **AI Assistance**: No AI ingredient normalization or recommendations.
* **Supabase Integration**: Currently stores changes to `localStorage` for mocking capabilities. Database triggers and real client queries will be wired in future development stages.
