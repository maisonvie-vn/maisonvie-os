# TASK-0023 — Recipe Foundation

## Objective
The objective of TASK-0023 is to implement the **Recipe Foundation** in MVOS Studio. This enables structured management of standard recipes and their respective ingredient lines, establishing a consistent cookery database for Maison Vie (BOH team) without introducing costing engines, margins, suppliers, inventory levels, or POS connections.

## Data Structure

### Recipe Fields
* `id` (UUID): Unique recipe identifier.
* `menuItemId` (UUID, Nullable): Optional foreign key to Menu Catalog item.
* `recipeCode` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Code of the recipe.
* `recipeName` (VARCHAR): Name of the recipe.
* `recipeType` (VARCHAR): Recipe type enums (`standard`, `prep`, `sauce`, `stock`, `garnish`, `pastry`, `beverage`, `staff_meal`, `other`).
* `status` (VARCHAR): Recipe status (`draft`, `active`, `paused`, `archived`).
* `version` (INTEGER): Recipe formulation version.
* `yieldQuantity` (NUMERIC, Nullable): Standard batch yield.
* `yieldUnit` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Yield measurement unit.
* `portionQuantity` (NUMERIC, Nullable): Standard portion size per guest.
* `portionUnit` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Portion measurement unit.
* `prepTimeMinutes` (INTEGER, Nullable): Preparation time.
* `cookTimeMinutes` (INTEGER, Nullable): Cook time.
* `difficultyLevel` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Difficulty enums (`low`, `medium`, `high`, `chef_only`).
* `method` (TEXT, Nullable): Production instructions.
* `platingNote` (TEXT, Nullable): Food presentation guidelines.
* `prepNote` (TEXT, Nullable): Ingredient preparation note.
* `serviceNote` (TEXT, Nullable): Service team note.
* `kitchenNote` (TEXT, Nullable): Chef's operational note.
* `allergyNote` (TEXT, Nullable): Allergen risk notes.
* `dietaryNote` (TEXT, Nullable): Dietary options.
* `storageNote` (TEXT, Nullable): Storage instructions.
* `ownerName` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Chef or staff responsible.
* `effectiveFrom` (DATE, Nullable).
* `effectiveTo` (DATE, Nullable).

### Recipe Ingredient Line Fields
* `id` (UUID): Unique ingredient line identifier.
* `recipeId` (UUID): Reference to parent recipe.
* `lineOrder` (INTEGER): Output sorting order.
* `ingredientName` (VARCHAR): Name of raw material or sub-recipe.
* `ingredientCategory` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Category enums (`protein`, `seafood`, `vegetable`, `fruit`, `dairy`, `dry_goods`, `spice`, `herb`, `sauce`, `stock`, `garnish`, `pastry`, `beverage`, `other`).
* `quantity` (NUMERIC, Nullable): Ingredient quantity.
* `unit` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Ingredient unit.
* `preparationNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `wasteNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `optional` (BOOLEAN): Flag indicating whether it's optional.

## Out of Scope & Limitations
* **Food Cost & rollup**: No recipe ingredient pricing, margin tracking, or yield loss costing.
* **Inventory Stock levels**: No link to real-time physical store inventory, purchase planning, or stock card movement.
* **POS Integration**: No sync to POS sale item codes.
* **AI Assistance**: No AI recipe generator or automatic recipe suggestions.
* **Supabase Integration**: Currently stores changes to `localStorage` for mocking capabilities. Database triggers and real client queries will be wired in future development stages.
