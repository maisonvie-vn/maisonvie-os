# TASK-0027 — Purchase Request Foundation

## Objective
The objective of TASK-0027 is to implement the **Purchase Request Foundation** in MVOS Studio. This enables Maison Vie to create structured internal purchase requests and lines, capturing kitchen or management procurement needs prior to purchasing orders, inventory receiving, or billing controls.

## Data Structure

### Purchase Request Header Fields
* `id` (UUID): Unique request identifier.
* `requestCode` (VARCHAR, Nullable): Request code (e.g., PR-20260628-01).
* `requestDate` (DATE): Date requested.
* `requestedBy` (VARCHAR): Requester name.
* `department` (VARCHAR): Department enum (`boh`, `foh`, `bar`, `reservation`, `management`, `finance`, `inventory`, `stewarding`, `housekeeping`, `maintenance`, `other`).
* `priority` (VARCHAR): Priority enum (`low`, `medium`, `high`, `urgent`).
* `status` (VARCHAR): Status enum (`draft`, `submitted`, `reviewing`, `approved_for_ordering`, `rejected`, `cancelled`, `closed`, `archived`).
* `neededByDate` (DATE, Nullable): Target fulfillment date.
* `preferredSupplierId` (UUID, Nullable): Link to preferred supplier.
* `reason` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `internalNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `submittedAt` (TIMESTAMPTZ, Nullable).
* `reviewedAt` (TIMESTAMPTZ, Nullable).
* `closedAt` (TIMESTAMPTZ, Nullable).
* `createdAt` (TIMESTAMPTZ).
* `updatedAt` (TIMESTAMPTZ).

### Purchase Request Line Fields
* `id` (UUID): Unique line identifier.
* `purchaseRequestId` (UUID): Reference to parent request.
* `lineOrder` (INT): Sorting order.
* `ingredientMasterId` (UUID, Nullable): Optional reference to Ingredient Master.
* `freeTextItemName` (TEXT, Nullable): Fallback name if no master ingredient is linked.
* `requestedQuantity` (NUMERIC, Nullable).
* `requestedUnit` (VARCHAR, Nullable).
* `preferredSupplierId` (UUID, Nullable): Optional supplier proposed for this line.
* `purposeNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `urgencyNote` (TEXT, Nullable).
* `status` (VARCHAR): Status enum (`open`, `reviewed`, `approved`, `rejected`, `cancelled`, `closed`).
* `createdAt` (TIMESTAMPTZ).
* `updatedAt` (TIMESTAMPTZ).

## Out of Scope & Limitations
* **Purchase Orders**: No purchase orders, procurement workflows, or supplier approval workflows.
* **Supplier Pricing**: No supplier price lists, price history, or contract pricing.
* **Costing & WAC**: No unit cost, WAC calculations, food cost, or recipe rollup.
* **Inventory & Stock**: No inventory integrations, stock counts, stock receiving, stock movements, or par stocks.
* **Accounting & Payments**: No invoice management, debt tracking, payment tracking, VAT calculations, or accounting integrations.
* **Supplier Selection**: No automatic supplier selection or supplier scoring.
* **AI & Optimization**: No AI purchase recommendations, forecasting, or production planning.
* **Multi-Branch Readiness**: No branch ownership, multi-branch filters, or branch permissions. RFC-0010 remains Proposed.
* **Supabase Integration**: Stores changes to `localStorage` for mocking capabilities.
