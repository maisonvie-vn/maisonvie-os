# SPEC_002: MVOS Business Capability Map Specification

This document outlines the core business capabilities enabled by the Maison Vie Operating System (MVOS).

---

## 1. Domain Capabilities

### Front of House (FOH)
- Guest welcoming protocols.
- Table seating and allocation queue.
- VIP reservation status routing.

### Back of House (BOH) & Culinary
- Taste testing adjustments with Chef Joel.
- Allergen catalog logs.
- Plating timing coordination.

### System & Operations
- Ambient sensory controls (lighting schedules, acoustic guidelines, room climate).
- Administrative policy management.
- Audit history logging.
