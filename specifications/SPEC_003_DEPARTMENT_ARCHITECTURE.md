# SPEC_003: MVOS Department Architecture Specification

This specification maps the organizational departments within the Maison Vie Operating System (MVOS).

---

## 1. Mapped Divisions
The system partitions roles and procedures into four departments:

1. **Administration**: Responsible for metadata settings, RLS rules, and local development diagnostic evaluations.
2. **Operations**: Coordinates lighting, temperature boundaries, and background classical acoustics.
3. **Front of House (FOH)**: Handles table assignments, reservation statuses, and guest welcoming procedures.
4. **Culinary**: Manages menu creation, ingredient checks, and food preparation workflows.
