# 🏛️ MVOS Business Architecture (001)

## 1. Organizational Hierarchy
Maison Vie operations are divided into distinct departments and roles to maintain luxury hospitality standards:

- **Administration**: Executive roles (Owner/CFO/Admin) managing settings, security policies, and technical system rules.
- **Front of House (FOH)**: Hostesses, sommeliers, and service staff coordinating reservations, table layouts, and guest welcomes.
- **Culinary**: Chef Joel (Bếp trưởng) and kitchen teams managing dining lists, allergen profiles, and course plating workflows.
- **Operations**: Technical leads controlling ambient parameters (lighting schedules, acoustics, private room climates).

## 2. Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) Flow
SOP files define formal rules that guide the actions of staff members:
- **Registry**: Approved guides are registered in the Document Library.
- **Lifecycle**: Documents transition from `DRAFT` to `REVIEW` and `APPROVED`.
- **Review Cycle**: Automated triggers alert owners of upcoming reviews based on the SOP's configured review frequency.
