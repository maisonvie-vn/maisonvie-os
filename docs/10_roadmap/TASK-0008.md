# TASK-0008 — CEO Dashboard Foundation

## Objective
The objective of TASK-0008 is to build the first **CEO Dashboard Foundation** inside MVOS Studio, providing a high-level operational overview to the CEO of Maison Vie. This dashboard aggregates live operational data from the existing **Reservation** and **Email** workflows, while clearly highlighting standard hospitality/restaurant domains that are not yet connected (Data Gaps) to prevent mock-up confusion.

## Features Implemented

### 1. Date Range Filters
Supports interactive filtering of reservation data via the following options:
* **Hôm nay** (`today`): Show bookings for `2026-06-28` (default).
* **7 ngày tới** (`next_7_days`): Show bookings within 7 days from `2026-06-28`.
* **30 ngày tới** (`next_30_days`): Show bookings within 30 days from `2026-06-28`.
* **Tháng này** (`current_month`): Show bookings scheduled for June 2026.

### 2. Live KPI Cards (Reservations & Emails)
* **Đặt bàn hôm nay**: Total count of reservations scheduled for today.
* **Số khách hôm nay**: Total sum of `partySize` for today's bookings.
* **Đoàn khách lớn sắp tới**: Total count of reservations with `partySize >= 6` scheduled for today or later.
* **Email chờ duyệt trả lời**: Count of email messages with drafts currently in the `Pending Review` status.
* **Đặt bàn chưa xác nhận**: Total count of reservations in `Draft` or `Pending` status.
* **Email nháp cần xử lý**: Total count of email messages with drafts in `Draft` or `Needs Revision` status.
* **Đặt bàn đã hủy**: Total count of reservations in `Cancelled` status.
* **Đặt bàn sắp tới**: Total count of reservations scheduled for future dates.

### 3. Reservation Overview Panel
Shows a filtered, scrollable table of reservations containing guest details, scheduling, party size, dining room, and status.

### 4. Email Workflow Panel
Displays count distribution of email replies by status (`Nháp`, `Chờ duyệt`, `Cần sửa`, `Đã duyệt`, `Đã gửi`) and lists unresolved drafts.

### 5. Operational Risks ("Cảnh báo vận hành")
Highlights critical operational warnings scanned dynamically from active datasets:
* Warns if today's reservations contain unconfirmed (`Draft` / `Pending`) bookings.
* Warns if drafts are awaiting FOH management approval (`Pending Review`).
* Warns of upcoming large group arrivals (`partySize >= 6`).
* Discloses that real-time POS revenue and food cost engine data are not connected.

### 6. Data Gaps Panel ("Dữ liệu chưa kết nối")
Explicitly lists missing operational domains not yet built on the frozen roadmap to avoid fake number reporting:
* **Doanh thu (Revenue)**: POS integration pending.
* **Chi phí nguyên liệu (Food Cost)**: Menu & Recipe module pending.
* **Chi phí nhân sự (Labor Cost)**: HR & Payroll module pending.
* **Hao hụt ẩm thực (Waste)**: Kitchen OS logging pending.
* **Đánh giá mạng xã hội (Reviews)**: Google Reviews/TripAdvisor API connection pending.
* **Báo cáo két sắt & quỹ (Cash)**: Cashier module connection pending.
* **Tồn kho & hầm rượu (Inventory)**: Inventory OS module pending.
* **Phân loại khách đoàn/lẻ (Guest Class)**: Missing categorization properties in the reservation model.

## Limitations & Future Roadmap
* **Database Connectivity**: Currently reads from client-side `localStorage` mirroring Supabase tables structure. Future tasks will connect this view to Supabase database listeners.
* **Authentication**: Access to this route will eventually be restricted to administrator/CEO role profiles.
